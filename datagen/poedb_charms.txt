        <div id="markContent"><h1 id="charm">Charm</h1>
<p>A charm is an item obtainable from Affliction encounters. Charms act functionally like jewels and must be socketed into a Charm Socket to activate its effects. To use a charm, a player must first choose the &quot;Wildwood Primalist&quot; Wildwood ascendancy class and allocate a Charm Socket ascendancy passive.</p>
<p>Tinctures are unmodifiable and are always found at magic rarity and can have up to two random modifiers based on stats from regular ascendancy classes. Each charm base type corresponds to a specific attribute and can only roll ascendancy modifiers from classes that possess that attribute.</p>
